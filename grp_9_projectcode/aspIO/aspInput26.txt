

School Kids Clean Up Creek!@@@@(EVERETT, WASHINGTON, June, 1988) - It has taken five years of hard work.@@@@But a group of school children have cleaned up Pigeon Creek.@@@@In 1983, the creek was so dirty that the fish had all died.@@@@A group of kids at Jackson School decided to do something about it.@@@@It became a project for their whole class.@@@@First Kids cleaned out the bottom of the creek, called the creek bed.@@@@There were many weeds.@@@@Kids also found a lot of trash and litter.@@@@Then the students raised fish eggs in a tank of water called an aquarium.@@@@When the eggs hatched, Kids emptied the new fish into the creek.@@@@Kids wanted others to stop making the creek dirty.@@@@So Kids made signs that said, "Don't Dump.@@@@Kids put the signs near the creek.@@@@Kids also made little books, called booklets.@@@@The booklets told people how to keep the creek clean.@@@@Now, the water is clean and new fish are being born in the creek.@@@@Pigeon Creek is a sign of the good things young people can do.@@@@1#  Who cleaned up the creek?@@@@2#  What was the name of the creek?@@@@3#  When did the work begin?@@@@4#  Where is the bed of a creek?@@@@5#  Why did the kids make booklets?
